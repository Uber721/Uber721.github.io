Commonly Used Equipment

This a Kadai. It is sort of like a wok. It is a deep, round bottomed pan, oerfect for frying, sauteing, and curries.

This is a tawa. It is a flat pan. It is used to make various flatbreads.

This is a pressure cooker. It is used to cook things like lentils, beans and rice quickly. A pressure cooker works by trapping steam inside a sealed pot, which increases the internal pressure and raises the water's boiling point from \(100^{\circ }C\) (\(212^{\circ }F\)) to about \(121^{\circ }C\) (\(250^{\circ }F\)). This higher temperature cooks food much faster than conventional methods

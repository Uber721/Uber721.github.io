Some helpful tips when using chillies =)

  • Size matters. Smaller chillies are generally hotter than larger ones
  • Wear gloves when handling chillies.
  • Most of a chillies heat is in their white inside bitties and their seeds.
  • Wash hands thouroughly when after operating chillies
There you go. Now you know some helpful tips when operating with chillies




What are Empanadas

Empanadas are basically supersized ravioli. They are small pockets of filling wrapped in pastry, usually in a triangular shape. When cooking, it is advised to poke holes in them, otherwise they might explode.

Some helpful tips when using chillies =)

  • Size matters. Smaller chillies are generally hotter than larger ones
  • Wear gloves when handling chillies.
  • Most of a chillies heat is in their white inside bitties and their seeds.
  • Wash hands thouroughly when after operating chillies
There you go. Now you know some helpful tips when operating with chillies




What are Empanadas

Empanadas are basically supersized ravioli. They are small pockets of filling wrapped in pastry, usually in a triangular shape. When cooking, it is advised to poke holes in them, otherwise they might explode.

Tapas Dishes

Patatas Bravas are just crispy potato pieces with sauces. Its like a different version of chips

Tortilla Espanolas are omelettes made with eggs, potatoes and onions. They are typically served in wedges

Gambas Al Ajillos are sizzling garlic shrimp cooked in olive oil with chilli




Why are Tapas Restaurants so popular?

Tapas restuarants are incredibly popular due to socially engaging atmosphere. This is because Tapas are designed for sharing. They come in many small dishes, which promotes charing and conversation. They also have an immense variety, so if somethings doesnt tickle your fancy, you can always just find another one to eat.
